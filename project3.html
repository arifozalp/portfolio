Sector Similarity Analysis of Stocks

An artificial intelligence project that analyzes time series behavior of stock prices across different sectors. The system identifies sectoral similarity of a stock and provides insights to support investment strategies.

What I Did:

  • Collected and processed financial time series data
  • Extracted features using tsfresh and statistical libraries
  • Trained and evaluated models using CatBoost and XGBoost

Technologies Used:

Python, NumPy, scikit-learn, tsfresh, CatBoost, XGBoost, Matplotlib
